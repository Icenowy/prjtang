
Welcome to Project Tang
=========================================

`Project Tang`_ documents the `Anlogic`_ FPGA architecture to enable
development of open-source tools.  Our goal is to provide sufficient information
to develop a free and open Verilog to bitstream toolchain for these devices.

.. _Project Tang: https://github.com/mmicko/prjtang
.. _Anlogic: http://www.anlogic.com/
